Echoes in the Night: The Sonder Story

Sonder isn't just music; it's an experience, a whispered confession in the dark, the space between city lights, the echoes of love and loss. Formed by Brent Faiyaz, Atu, and Dpat, Sonder merges silky R&B with lush, immersive production, creating sonic landscapes that resonate deep within.

Brent, the voice of fleeting moments, crafts lyrics that feel like late-night journal entries. Atu, the architect of emotion, laces beats with nostalgia. And Dpat, the mastermind behind the atmosphere, stitches it all together with cinematic finesse. Every note, every pause, every breath is intentional.

Sonder crafts atmospheric R&B infused with raw emotion, hypnotic melodies, and deep, immersive soundscapes. Their music is an intimate reflection of longing, love, and fleeting moments.
